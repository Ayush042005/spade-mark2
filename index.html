About SPADE

SPADE (Society for Practical Applications and Development of Economics) is the economics society of Shivaji College, University of Delhi. Since our inception in 2017, we have been committed to fostering a vibrant community that explores economics beyond the classroom.

📈

Events & Workshops

We organize multiple online and offline events, engaging over 10,000 students and collaborating with 300+ societies from Delhi University, IIMs, and beyond.

🎬

Documentaries

We have produced 5 thought-provoking documentaries on urgent economic issues, with our 6th currently in production, aiming to spark conversations and inspire change.

📚

Publications

We proudly publish "Arthamanthan," our annual ISBN-approved research book, and "Tatva," our annual magazine, to showcase student research and perspectives.

Our Publications

Annual Magazine

Tatva

Our annual magazine, Tatva, is a thoughtful compilation of ideas and perspectives. The latest edition explores Neuroeconomics, featuring 21 research articles from students across 20 institutions.

Annual Research Book

Arthamanthan

Our esteemed annual research book, "Arthamanthan," features a diverse collection of research papers on various socio-economic issues in India and has earned an ISBN for the past two years.

Annual Documentary

The Rise of the Gig Economy

Our latest documentary explores how short-term, freelance, and contract work is reshaping employment, featuring interviews with eminent personalities and experts.
